Rent split across roommates
Residents can split a rent charge across roommates from the charge detail screen — a split editor, per-roommate share calculation, and a check that shares sum to 100%. The spec capped any single resident's share at 90%, but the cap moved to a server-side check late in the build (divergence #2) — that's the highest-risk area to verify.
